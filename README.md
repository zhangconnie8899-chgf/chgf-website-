# Chinese Heritage Guardians Fund (CHGF)

## Time on the Wall

Chinese Heritage Guardians Fund (CHGF) is a federally incorporated Canadian not-for-profit organization dedicated to advancing public education on Chinese cultural heritage through exhibitions, research, digital storytelling, AI innovation, and youth engagement.

---

### Mission

To reconnect people with Chinese cultural heritage preserved around the world through research, exhibitions, museum collaboration, education, and AI-powered storytelling.

---

### Current Project

**Time on the Wall**

A public education initiative exploring Chinese cultural heritage preserved in museums around the world.
